How Many People Use Social Media in 2022

Social media’s infiltration into the lives of internet users has been on the rise. The latest figures show that there are expected to be 3.96 billion social media users worldwide in 2022—a 4.8 percent increase from a year ago. It is also 1.1 billion more than the number of social users in 2017, which represents a whopping 38.5 percent jump in just six years. The average annual growth rate over this period is 6.7 percent.
